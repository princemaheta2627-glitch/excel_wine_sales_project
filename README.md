# 🍷 Wine Sales Performance Analysis

A comprehensive Data Analytics project analyzing four years of wine sales data (2010–2013) to uncover revenue trends, regional performance, product insights, and logistics efficiency using Microsoft Excel and Python.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project simulates the responsibilities of a Data Analyst working for an Australian wine distribution company. Using transactional sales data, I performed data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), PivotTable reporting, dashboard creation, and extended analysis with Python to identify business trends and actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project was to answer key business questions such as:

- How has revenue changed over time?
- Which wine segments and regions generate the highest revenue?
- Which products and wine varietals perform best?
- How efficient are the shipping operations?
- Which packaging types contribute the most sales?

# 📊 Analysis Performed

## 1️⃣ Revenue Trend Analysis

Analyzed yearly revenue growth from 2010 to 2013.

### Result

- Revenue increased from **$1.97M** to **$2.94M**
- Overall growth of **48.9%**
- Highest annual growth occurred in **2013**

---

## 2️⃣ Revenue by Wine Segment

Compared revenue across wine categories.

### Result

| Wine Segment | Revenue |
|--------------|---------:|
| Rose / Sparkling Wine | $3.78M |
| White Wine | $3.21M |
| Red Wine | $2.26M |

**Top Segment:** Rose / Sparkling Wine

---

## 3️⃣ Regional Performance

Analyzed sales by region.

### Result

| Region | Revenue |
|---------|---------:|
| South | $3.35M |
| East | $2.45M |
| West | $2.34M |
| North | $1.09M |

**Best Performing Region:** South

---

## 4️⃣ Product Performance

Identified the highest revenue-generating products.

### Top Products

- Robert Oatley Sauvignon Blanc 2013
- Orin Swift Cellars Fragile Rose 2014
- Gerard Bertrand Cote des Roses Rose 2014

**Highest Revenue Product**

- Robert Oatley Sauvignon Blanc 2013
- Revenue: **$392K**

---

## 5️⃣ Top Wine Varietals

Highest-performing varietals based on revenue.

- Pinot Gris
- Reisling
- Sauvignon Blanc
- Champagne
- Chardonnay

**Top Varietal:** Pinot Gris ($1.06M)

---

## 6️⃣ Shipping & Logistics Analysis

Evaluated freight modes and delivery efficiency.

### Key Findings

- Express Road Cargo handled **74.7%** of orders.
- Average delivery time was approximately **2 days**.
- Express Road Cargo had the lowest freight cost ratio (**1.13% of revenue**).

---

## 7️⃣ Packaging Analysis

Compared packaging types by revenue contribution.

### Top Packaging

- Large Crate
- Extra Large Crate

Large Crates generated approximately **$3.30M**, making them the highest-performing packaging type.

---

## 8️⃣ State-wise Revenue Analysis

| State | Revenue |
|--------|---------:|
| New South Wales | $2.56M |
| Western Australia | $2.18M |
| South Australia | $0.96M |
| Victoria | $0.88M |

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Revenue increased by nearly **49%** over four years.
- Rose/Sparkling Wine generated the highest revenue.
- The South region contributed over one-third of total sales.
- Pinot Gris was the best-performing wine varietal.
- Express Road Cargo proved to be the most efficient shipping method.
- Large Crates were the dominant packaging type.
- New South Wales generated the highest state revenue.
